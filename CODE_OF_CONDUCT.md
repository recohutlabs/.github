# Code of Conduct

Recohut Labs is committed to a welcoming, professional, and technically rigorous community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and specific when giving feedback.
- Critique ideas and evidence, not people.
- Welcome questions and different levels of experience.
- Disclose conflicts of interest and respect privacy.
- Accept responsibility, apologize, and repair harm when mistakes happen.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct, deliberate intimidation, doxxing, and sustained disruption are not tolerated in project spaces or related community interactions.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to an organization owner through GitHub. Maintainers may edit or remove content, restrict participation, or ban contributors when needed to protect the community. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.

Enforcement decisions should consider context, impact, prior behavior, and whether harm was repaired.
